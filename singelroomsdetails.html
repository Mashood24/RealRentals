About This Room

This cozy single room is located in a secure apartment complex in the heart of Westlands, Nairobi. Perfect for working professionals or students, the room offers a comfortable living space with all essential amenities included.

The room comes fully furnished with a single bed, study desk, wardrobe, and bedside table. Large windows provide plenty of natural light and a pleasant view of the surrounding area.

As part of a shared apartment, you'll have access to a fully equipped communal kitchen, modern bathroom facilities, and a comfortable lounge area. The building features 24/7 security, CCTV surveillance, and a backup generator for uninterrupted power supply.
