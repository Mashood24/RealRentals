About This Retail Space

This premium mall retail unit is located on the ground floor of Two Rivers Mall, Nairobi's largest shopping destination. With over 800 sq.ft of customizable space, it's perfect for fashion boutiques, electronics stores, or specialty retail.

The space comes with standard mall finishes including tiled floors, drop ceiling with recessed lighting, and a storefront glass facade. The unit has been pre-wired for POS systems and security cameras.

Two Rivers Mall attracts over 50,000 visitors weekly, with peak traffic on weekends and holidays. The mall features ample parking, 24/7 security, and a diverse mix of anchor tenants including Carrefour, Nike, and LC Waikiki.
