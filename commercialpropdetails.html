About This Property

This premium office space in Westlands offers a prestigious business address with high-end finishes and modern amenities. The 2,500 sq.ft suite is located on the 8th floor of a Class A office building with 24/7 security and professional management.

The space features an open floor plan that can accommodate 15-20 workstations, two private offices, a conference room, and a reception area. Floor-to-ceiling windows provide abundant natural light and panoramic views of Nairobi.

The building offers premium amenities including a business center, conference facilities, on-site café, and ample parking. Located in the heart of Westlands, this property provides easy access to major banks, restaurants, and transportation hubs.
