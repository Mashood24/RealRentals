Who We Are

Founded in 2015, cREN Rentals began as a small team passionate about solving Kenya's housing challenges. What started as a modest operation in Nairobi has grown into one of Kenya's most trusted rental platforms, serving thousands of happy tenants across the country.

Our journey began when our founder, John Mwangi, struggled to find affordable, quality housing in Nairobi. Recognizing this widespread challenge, he assembled a team of real estate professionals and tech experts to create a solution that would transform Kenya's rental market.

Today, we operate in 12 counties across Kenya, with over 5,000 properties listed on our platform. We've helped more than 20,000 Kenyans find their perfect homes while maintaining a 98% tenant satisfaction rate.

Our Mission

To revolutionize Kenya's rental housing market by providing innovative, transparent, and accessible housing solutions that empower both tenants and property owners through technology and exceptional service.

Our Vision

To become East Africa's most trusted housing platform, where every individual can find quality, affordable housing with ease, dignity, and peace of mind by 2030.
