About Me

With over 5 years of experience in Nairobi's luxury real estate market, I specialize in helping clients find their dream apartments in Westlands and surrounding areas. My deep understanding of the local market dynamics ensures my clients get the best value for their investment.

I take pride in building long-term relationships with my clients, many of whom have become repeat customers and friends. My approach combines market expertise, attention to detail, and personalized service to make the rental process seamless and stress-free.

When I'm not helping clients find their perfect home, you can find me exploring Nairobi's vibrant restaurant scene or volunteering with local community housing initiatives.
